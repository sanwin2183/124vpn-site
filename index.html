Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026  ·  Effective Date: April 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to 124VPN ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (hashed) when you create an account.
  • Payment Information: Billing transactions are processed by our third-party payment provider. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Diagnostic Data: Anonymous crash reports and app performance metrics to maintain service quality. These contain no personally identifiable information.
  • Device Information: Operating system type and version, solely for compatibility and support purposes.

🔒 No-Logs Guarantee: We do not collect, monitor, log, or store your browsing activity, DNS queries, IP addresses, connection timestamps, session duration, bandwidth used, or any traffic data. Your online activity is your own.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect or store:

  • Your real IP address or the IP address of servers you connect to
  • Your browsing history, websites visited, or DNS queries
  • Content of your internet traffic or communications
  • Connection timestamps, session duration, or bandwidth consumption
  • Your physical location beyond what you voluntarily provide

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and send transactional emails (receipts, subscription reminders)
  • Respond to support requests you initiate
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the Service
  • Improve app stability via anonymous crash diagnostics

We do not use your information for advertising, behavioral profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data with third parties only in these limited circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: To complete billing transactions securely.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosting providers that store account data under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by a valid court order or applicable law. Because we hold no traffic logs, any such order would yield no user activity data.

6. Data Retention

Account information is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Anonymous diagnostic data is retained for no more than 90 days.

7. Security

We protect your data using industry-standard measures including AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest, strict access controls, and regular security audits. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; however, we take all commercially reasonable steps to protect your information.

8. Children's Privacy

124VPN is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@124vpn.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including the use of Standard Contractual Clauses where required by GDPR.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, disclose, or sell. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at privacy@124vpn.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our Privacy Team:

  • Email: privacy@124vpn.com
  • Support: support@124vpn.com
